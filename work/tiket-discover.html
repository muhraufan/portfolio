The Beginning

Once upon a time, people opened tiket.com only when they already knew where to go. They came for discounts, not discovery.

But travel doesn't start with booking — it starts with a spark. A photo, a story, a "maybe someday." That spark was scattered across apps, blogs, and social media — never inside tiket.com.

We saw an opportunity:

What if the journey could begin here?

The Ending

Discover turned tiket.com from a booking app into a place where travel begins. It united product, marketing, and content teams around one shared story — built on collaboration, flexibility, and clarity.

It reminded me that great design isn't only about solving user pain points; it's about aligning teams, empowering them to create, and letting the product grow beyond its surface.

Impact & Achievements

  • Increased Daily Active Users (DAU) by transforming tiket.com into a content and discovery hub, not just a booking platform.
  • Enabled faster brand collaborations — the marketing team could easily pitch thematic, centralized experiences to partners and influencers.
  • Improved internal workflows — content teams now manage blogs, promotions, and placements within a single Discover internal tools.
  • Boosted revenue streams — growth not only from bookings, but also from brand partnerships and sponsored content.
