About

A few deeper things about me.

Senior Product Designer based in Tokyo with a love for film photography.

I'm Raufan, a Digital Product Designer and Film Photographer based in Tokyo with over a decade of experience shaping digital experiences across travel, e-commerce, and news.

My design approach is rooted in versatility — working across multiple industries and cultural landscapes has honed my ability to craft intuitive, user-centered solutions that feel natural and seamless. I thrive on tackling complex problems, simplifying them into meaningful interactions that enhance how people engage with technology.

Beyond digital design, my passion for film photography allows me to explore storytelling in a different form. Capturing landscapes and everyday moments through film has deepened my appreciation for detail, composition, and the beauty of imperfection — principles that also influence my design philosophy.

I believe great design is not just about aesthetics but about understanding people, bridging perspectives, and creating impact. Whether through a product that simplifies a journey or a photograph that evokes emotion, my goal is to craft experiences that leave a lasting impression.
